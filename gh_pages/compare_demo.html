Which image best preserves content and style?

I. Test Description

Consider the reference images, denominated Content and Style images, shown below. Based on them, five different images, denominated Image 1 to Image 5, are generated such that they keep content of the Content Image and the style of the Style image. Take a close look at these images and provide a ranking from 1 to 5 , where 1 is the image that Worst depicts the content and style of the reference images and 5 is the image that Best depicts the content and style of the reference images. Your score should take into consideration the following profiles:

  • Are the objects from the content image clearly depicted?
  • Are the object textures and color pallette from the style image preserved?
  • Do the image suffers from distorsions or inconsistent shadowing?

Additionally, please include any comments regarding your choice.
